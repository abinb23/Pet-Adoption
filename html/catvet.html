    FAQs About Pet Veterinary

  • A video call works the same way as a WhatsApp video or Facetime call. You will be guided step by step before starting the call so that the veterinary doctor will be able to see your pet and observe any behavior or physical signs that will help them give the best guidance. If you opt for an online vet chat consultation, it will work the same way as a WhatsApp or Messenger chat. Good for those times when you're at work.

  • Each Video consultation is estimated to last 20 minutes, but if we are unable to deliver the solution you need in that time the vet can extend.

  • Yes, if the vet determines that your pet needs medication, they will provide a prescription during the online consultation. You can then purchase the prescribed medication from a pharmacy or an online pet store. The vet may also give you instructions on how to administer the medication.

  • Before your online vet consultation, ensure your pet is calm and in a quiet environment. Have any relevant medical records, a list of symptoms, and questions ready for the vet. Make sure your device is fully charged, and the camera and microphone are working properly for the best consultation experience.
